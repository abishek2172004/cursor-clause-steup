# Sources: AI-Powered SEO Content Production Experts

This file lists the 10 experts selected for this research, why each was chosen,
and where to find their primary content channels. Selection criteria: each
person had to be a genuine **practitioner** — someone who runs real AI-assisted
content/SEO operations (their own or clients') and publishes evidence of it
(data, screenshots, case studies) — not just a commentator repeating SEO-tool
marketing copy.

Research conducted: June 2026.

---

## 1. Jake Ward
- **Role:** Co-founder, Byword (AI content platform) & Kleo; ran an SEO agency
  generating $20M+/year for clients before that.
- **Why selected:** Built and scaled an AI content tool from the inside —
  posts real Google Search Console screenshots, revenue numbers, and
  before/after traffic data rather than generic advice. One of his most-cited
  posts documents hitting 1M/mo SEO traffic from ~7,000 AI-generated pages.
- **LinkedIn:** https://www.linkedin.com/in/jakezward
- **Website:** https://jakeward.io/
- **Annotation:** Best source for the *practitioner's playbook* on building
  and scaling an AI content product, plus honest commentary on what
  AI content can and can't do for conversions.

## 2. Mark Williams-Cook
- **Role:** Director at Candour (SEO agency); founder of AlsoAsked, QueryFan,
  IntentGaps, CoreUpdates; 20+ years in search.
- **Why selected:** Runs hands-on, public experiments comparing AI-generated
  vs. scraped content at scale (e.g., a 10,000-page zero-human-editing test),
  and discovered a Google ranking-signals exploit that earned him a bug
  bounty. Publishes via Substack, YouTube, and the Search with Candour podcast.
- **YouTube:** https://www.youtube.com/@SearchwithCandour (and guest
  appearances, e.g. https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8humkUT8w9k)
- **Substack:** https://markwilliamscook.substack.com/
- **Annotation:** Best source for *rigorous, skeptical experimentation* —
  he tests claims rather than repeating them, which is valuable for a
  playbook that needs to separate hype from results.

## 3. Eli Schwartz
- **Role:** SEO advisor; author of *Product-Led SEO*; ex-SurveyMonkey SEO
  lead (grew organic from ~0 to a major channel; helped scale the company
  toward $100M+ in revenue via SEO); has worked with Zapier, Tinder,
  Coinbase, Quora, WordPress.
- **Why selected:** Direct relevance to B2B SaaS — his core thesis is treating
  SEO as a product function, and his current newsletter focuses specifically
  on how AI is changing that for SaaS companies.
- **Newsletter:** https://www.productledseo.com/ (Substack)
- **LinkedIn:** https://www.linkedin.com/in/schwartze
- **Annotation:** Best source for *product-led SEO strategy* in a B2B SaaS
  context specifically, plus a credible "AI is disrupting old content
  models" perspective from someone who isn't selling an AI tool.

## Notes on what was excluded

Several names that show up at the top of generic "Top 10/50/150 SEO
Experts" listicles (e.g., Neil Patel, Brian Dean, Rand Fishkin) were
deliberately excluded. Per their own current bios, they have each largely
moved on from hands-on SEO practice into adjacent businesses, education, or
unrelated ventures — they were "famous," not necessarily "current
practitioners," which the brief specifically asked to avoid.
